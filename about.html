The Story

breathe: to pause, as for breath; to take rest

breathe is an environment dedicated to essential need to pause and take in the beauty of moments that we stumble upon, yet that often go unnoticed.

However, this site is also dedicated to helping readers to explore the English language in an informal setting. Develop a community, rely on each other and strengthen each other through a positive outlook on taking time away from stress and anxiety.

Together we will explore an alternative lifestyle that eases the mind and helps us to improve our perspectives of personal boundaries.
